## 1. AUKUS

Two pillars: **I** (nuclear subs) and **II** (AI, quantum, hypersonics, cyber). The US DoD **review (Jun 2025)** rattled Canberra; **Trump signaled support (Oct 2025)**. The program was **revised**: Australia now expects **three second-hand US Virginia-class** boats plus the British-designed **SSN-AUKUS** build; it paid the UK **A$310M (Feb 2026)** for long-lead propulsion items. The high-risk transition timeline + dependence on US/UK industrial capacity are the stress points. *Fact.*

## 2. The critical-minerals pivot

Australia is positioning as the West's alternative to Chinese mineral dominance: the **US-Australia Framework** (Oct 2025, Albanese-Trump) + a **A$1.2B Critical Minerals Strategic Reserve** (antimony/gallium/rare earths). **Lynas** became the **first company outside China** to make commercial **dysprosium oxide** (May 2025; Mount Weld feedstock) — a genuine crack in China's heavy-rare-earth monopoly. Pairs with **MP Materials** (the US side). *Fact.*

## 3. The iron-ore China dependence (the counterweight)

Australia's largest export goes overwhelmingly to China, and **China is wielding buyer (monopsony) power**: in 2025 it **centralized iron-ore purchasing** (China Mineral Resources Group) and imposed **brand restrictions** (BHP's Jinbao — ~**$2.8B**/yr hit); **Rio & Fortescue switched their China pricing benchmark** off the S&P Platts index. **The state Australia is arming against (AUKUS) is its dominant customer.** *Fact.*

## 4. The super pool

A **~A$4T** retirement-savings pool (Superannuation Guarantee at **12%** from Jul 2025; **Division 296** extra tax on >$3M balances from Jul 2026) — one of the world's largest pension pools and a major institutional-capital force. *Fact.*

## 5. The quantum bet

Commonwealth + Queensland committed **A$940M** to **PsiQuantum's** utility-scale quantum computer near Brisbane (Moreton Bay; Lendlease; ground broken Jun 2026; completion targeted end-2027) — already a node in the quantum-landscape block; plus Silicon Quantum Computing, Diraq, Quantum Brilliance. *Commitment fact; timeline slipped (contested execution).*
