# Planet Labs & the commercial space-imaging / Earth-observation industry — operators, the NRO/NGA demand side, launch, and the 2025-26 consolidation (documented, dated)

*Built 2026-07-02 from `research/spec-planet-space-imaging.json`. Overlay on the geopolitics-space-layer / defense-industrial-base threads. Cross-links `NRO`, `NGA`, `SDA`, `US_Government`, `IonQ`, `Tether`, `SpaceX`/Starshield, `Google`.*

> **Frame.** The commercial Earth-observation (EO) layer that feeds US/allied intelligence and the AI-analytics economy. **Planet Labs** (NYSE: PL) runs the largest daily-scan fleet (Dove/SuperDove) plus tasking (SkySat→Pelican) and hyperspectral (Tanager). The field churned hard in 2025-26 — **Maxar → Vantor + Lanteris** (Oct 1 2025, Advent-owned); **Capella → IonQ** (Jul 15 2025); **HawkEye 360**, **SpaceX**, and **Firefly** IPO'd; **Spire** sold AIS to Kpler; **Albedo** exited imagery. Demand is bifurcated: the **NRO buys the pixels** (EOCL), the **NGA does analytics/distribution** (Luno; the GEGD portal; the Ukraine surge).
>
> **Discipline.** IDIQ "ceilings" are competed maxima, **not** guaranteed revenue; "launched" ≠ "operational"; headline GAAP losses can be non-cash. Facts are sourced; secondary/aggregator figures and forward guidance are flagged. Overlay; excluded from the proofs.

## 1. Planet Labs (fact)

Co-founders **Will Marshall** (CEO) + **Robbie Schingler**; gov work via **Planet Labs Federal, Inc.** Constellations: **PlanetScope** (Dove/SuperDove, daily ~3m 8-band, continuously replenished — 36 launched Jan 2025 + 36 Nov 2025); **SkySat** (~50cm tasking, ex-Google Terra Bella 2017); **Pelican** (SkySat successor, ~50→30cm, ~8 on orbit by May 2026); **Tanager** (hyperspectral/methane with Carbon Mapper; Tanager-1 Aug 2024, 11,000+ plumes); **"Owl"** (announced Oct 7 2025, near-daily 1m, onboard NVIDIA GPUs). **AI pivot:** Anthropic/Claude (Mar 2025), NVIDIA "GPU-native AI engine" (Mar 2026, ran inference in space on a Pelican Apr 2026), Planet Insights Platform. **FY2026** (ended Jan 31 2026): revenue **$307.7M (+26%)**, **first full-year positive Adj EBITDA +$15.5M**, GAAP net loss $(246.9)M of which ~**$161.4M is a non-cash warrant revaluation**, cash+ST **$640.1M**, backlog **$900.4M (+79%)**. *Fact (FY26 via 8-K; SkySat op-count & FY27 guidance flagged secondary).*

## 2. Planet's contracts & ownership (fact; the "one big NGA check" premise is wrong)

Biggest deals are **commercial/allied-sovereign**, not one NGA award: **SKY Perfect JSAT (Japan) $230M** (Jan 2025, dedicated ~10-sat Pelican — commercial, not JAXA); **Germany €240M/~$280M** (Jul 2025, security); **Sweden** low-nine-figure sovereign military (Jan 2026). US intel: **NRO EOCL** (2022, initial **~$146M** — an initial award, not a 10-yr ceiling); **NGA Luno B** slot (Jan 2025, 8-figure task orders). Ownership: **Alphabet/Google** largest single holder (~10-11%, legacy Terra Bella — *flag: verify % vs 13F/proxy*).

## 3. The landscape (fact; corrections dated)

- **Maxar → Vantor (intel) + Lanteris (space)** (rebrand Oct 1 2025; Advent-owned): WorldView Legion 30cm; **largest NRO EOCL ~$3.24B**; runs NGA **GEGD**; primary Ukraine imagery; Raptor/Tensorglobe AI.
- **BlackSky** (BKSY): Gen-3 35cm on Rocket Lab; Spectra AI; **NRO EOCL ~$1.02B**; Luno A/B.
- **Satellogic** (SATL): ~18-19 NewSats (not "50+"); **Tether's $30M is convertible debt**; **Mnuchin's Liberty Strategic largest equity holder (27.39%)**, Gen. Dunford director.
- **Capella Space** (SAR) **acquired by IonQ** (closed Jul 15 2025); first US commercial SAR to work with NRO (2019).
- **Umbra** (SAR): 25cm routine; NRO SCE Stage III; SpaceWERX STRATFI up to $60M.
- **ICEYE** (Finland, SAR): largest commercial SAR (**62 launched**); **Series F >€1B at ~$12B** (Jun 2026); sells **sovereign systems** (Germany/Rheinmetall JV ~$2B); heavy Ukraine role.
- **Spire** (SPIR): sold AIS/maritime to **Kpler** ($233.5M, Apr 2025); now GNSS-RO weather/aviation.
- **HawkEye 360** (HAWK): **IPO'd May 7 2026** (~$2.4B); largest commercial RF-geolocation constellation; NRO anchor; FY25 first full-year profit.
- **Albedo**: VLEO 10cm (highest commercial res) but **exited commercial imagery ~Oct 2025** to build VLEO buses (Clarity-1 reentered Jun 10 2026).
- **Airbus Defence & Space** (AIR): Pléiades Neo (**2 of 4**; 5/6 lost in the Vega-C 2022 failure); **"Project Bromo"** MoU to merge Airbus/Leonardo/Thales space (Oct 23 2025, target 2027).
- **Synspective** (Japan SAR): public (Tokyo 290A, Dec 2024); Japan MoD slot.
- **China:** **ChangGuang/Jilin-1** (state-linked; world's largest sub-meter commercial EO; **OFAC-sanctioned Dec 2023** for Wagner imagery; new Iran-related OFAC sanction **May 8 2026**; an Apr 2025 State item was a *warning* not a sanction); **Spacety** (**OFAC-sanctioned Jan 2023** for supplying Ukraine SAR to a Wagner-linked firm).

## 4. The government demand side (fact)

**NRO buys the pixels; NGA does analytics/distribution.** NRO **EOCL** (10-yr 2022-32): Vantor ~$3.24B, BlackSky ~$1.02B, Planet ~$146M initial. NRO **SCE** by phenomenology (SAR: Capella/ICEYE US/Umbra to Jan 2027; RF; hyperspectral; EO; new CSO approach 2026). NGA **Luno A** ($290M, Sept 2024, 10 vendors) + **Luno B** ($200M, Jan 2025, 13 vendors incl. Planet Labs Federal) — ~$490M combined; 8-figure task orders. **Ukraine/COCOMs:** NGA more than doubled commercial-EO buys via the **GEGD** portal (prime Vantor); Ukraine access suspended Mar 7 2025, restored ~Mar 18 2025 (policy-driven). **pLEO:** SDA's PWSA (Tranche 1 first launch Sept 2025); NRO's own proliferated recon uses **SpaceX Starshield** (with Northrop). *Fact; the "$1.8B 2021 Starshield" figure is Reuters-reported (flagged).*

## 5. Launch layer & consolidation (fact)

**SpaceX** (SPCX, IPO'd Jun 2026): Falcon 9 + Transporter rideshare = EO-smallsat workhorse; Starshield = NRO proliferated recon. **Rocket Lab** (RKLB): Electron = dedicated small-EO/SAR launcher; moving up-stack (Neutron slipped to Q4 2026; $816M SDA prime; bought Geost; own constellation planned). **ULA**: Vulcan **halted** after a Feb 12 2026 SRB anomaly (CEO Bruno → Blue Origin). **Blue Origin**: New Glenn booster landing Nov 2025, then an **LC-36 pad explosion May 28 2026**. **Firefly** (FLY, IPO'd Aug 2025; Blue Ghost lunar landing Mar 2025). **Sierra Space** ($550M Series C at $8B, Mar 2026). **Consolidation:** RF SIGINT → HawkEye 360 (absorbed Aurora Insight/Maxar RF + ISA); SAR bifurcating (Capella→IonQ; ICEYE selling sovereign systems); EU imaging/manufacturing → the Airbus-Leonardo-Thales "Bromo" entity.

## 6. The honest reading

Commercial space-imaging is now a **strategic ISR layer**: a few EO operators (Vantor, Planet, BlackSky), SAR operators (Capella/ICEYE/Umbra), and RF (HawkEye 360) sell into a **bifurcated US demand structure** — NRO imagery (EOCL/SCE) + NGA analytics (Luno) distributed via GEGD — and the Ukraine war (and the Mar 2025 suspend-then-restore) shows how **load-bearing and policy-contingent** the commercial layer has become. Planet's niche is **daily** global coverage + a fast AI/analytics pivot, now at its first full year of positive Adjusted EBITDA. Three recurring traps the sources themselves flag: **(1)** IDIQ "ceilings" ≠ revenue (Maxar's $3.24B is not Planet's ~$146M); **(2)** "launched" ≠ "operational" (ICEYE 62 launched; Jilin-1 117 claimed vs ~63 trackable); **(3)** GAAP losses can be non-cash (Planet's warrant revaluation; Satellogic's Tether-note swings). Ownership threads for the wider graph: **IonQ→Capella** (quantum+SAR), **Tether + Mnuchin's Liberty Strategic→Satellogic**, **Advent→Vantor**, **Google's ~10% of Planet**.
