# Anomalous deaths & disappearances in strategically sensitive domains

*Built 2026-06-14. Verified against Meduza, Reuters/BBC/AP/NYT/NPR, the Charleston County Coroner, RFE/RL & USIP's Iran Primer, Newsweek/SCMP-sourced reporting, and the US House Select Committee on the CCP. Full URLs at the bottom.*

> **Discipline first — this is a topic engineered for conspiracy, so the method is the point.** The honest finding is **not** "one hidden hand is killing everyone." It is that there are **several distinct clusters, each with a different evidentiary status**, and the value is in *refusing to collapse them into one story*. Every case below is graded by **attribution quality**. No unified campaign is asserted, and **no person or state is accused beyond what a court, coroner, government, or major outlet already states.**

## 0. The base-rate floor (read this before any list "looks" sinister)
- **Denominators are huge.** Thousands of senior Russian energy executives, thousands of crypto founders, tens of thousands of China-affiliated defense scientists. In populations that large, a steady stream of heart attacks, cancers, accidents, and suicides is *statistically expected*. A list of N "striking" deaths is meaningless without the base rate.
- **Lists are curated after the fact** to include only the deaths that *look* suspicious (young, sudden, prominent) and exclude the ordinary majority — textbook selection bias plus the human tendency to over-detect patterns in random clustering.
- **What would actually elevate a cluster:** (a) a common, specific antecedent (all just testified; all on one program), (b) implausible cause-of-death mechanics, (c) official attribution, or (d) a credible actor with motive **and** demonstrated capability. Those tests are applied per-strand.

## 1. Russian energy & finance executives (2022– ) — *real cluster, cause unestablished*
The most statistically striking civilian cluster. Independent outlet **Meduza** (Jul 2025) reviewed the sudden deaths of **~13 Russian energy and transportation executives since 2022**; broader "suspicious deaths of Russian businesspeople" tallies run higher. Firms touched: **Gazprom, Lukoil, Transneft, Novatek, Gazprombank**.
- **Named cases:** Ravil Maganov (Lukoil chairman, fell from a hospital window, Sep 2022); Leonid Shulman & Alexander Tyulyakov (Gazprom units, Jan/Feb 2022); Vladislav Avayev (Gazprombank VP, official "murder-suicide", Apr 2022); Vladimir Nekrasov (Lukoil chairman, "acute heart failure", Oct 2023); Andrei Badalov (Transneft VP, fell from a window, Jul 2025).
- **Attribution:** *contested — no established perpetrator.* No body has tied these to a coordinated campaign. Hypotheses span Kremlin/elite enforcement, sanctions-driven ruin and suicide, business/organized-crime disputes, and coincidence. Russian official causes are widely distrusted given the information environment but are **not** independently disproven. *Grade: fact (deaths) + contested (cause).*

## 2. Iranian nuclear scientists (2007– ) — *the one strand with formal attribution*
The clearest case where "scientists keep dying" has a documented mechanism and an attributed actor.
- **Mohsen Fakhrizadeh**, Iran's top nuclear scientist ("the brains"), assassinated near Tehran **27 Nov 2020** with a remote-operated, AI-assisted machine gun (NYT). At least the **fifth** Iranian nuclear-linked scientist killed or dead in suspicious circumstances since ~2007.
- **Earlier:** Masoud Ali Mohammadi (2010, motorcycle bomb), Majid Shahriari (2010), Darioush Rezaeinejad (2011), Mostafa Ahmadi Roshan (2012, Natanz supervisor, magnetic car bomb).
- **Attribution:** widely reported as an **Israeli** covert-action campaign (Israel historically does not claim such operations). *Grade: fact (killings) + labeled interpretation (attribution).*

## 3. Chinese defense / microelectronics / military-AI researchers — *reported cluster, unverified, no perpetrator*
- **Reported deaths:** ~10 Chinese scientists flagged in suspicious circumstances, several in 2023, working on drones, military AI, microelectronics, and **Taiwan-invasion simulations** — e.g., **Feng Yanghe** (defense-tech professor, car crash, Jul 2023, reportedly working on Taiwan-conflict simulations); **Zhou Guangyuan** (chemist, died at 51, Dec 2023, no cause given); **Zhang Xiaoxin** (space expert, reported car accident, Dec 2024).
- **Disappearances are a *different* phenomenon:** senior PRC tech-defense officials have been **detained in anti-corruption / loyalty purges** (e.g., **Chen Zhaoxiong**, ex-chairman of China Electronics Technology Group, 2024) — documented internal state action, not unexplained death.
- **Attribution:** reporting **explicitly states no evidence links** the Chinese cases to US cases or to each other. Plausible: accidents/illness, internal security actions, selection bias. *Grade: contested → unsupported (no perpetrator established).* (Intersects [[geopolitics-chip-chokepoint-war]] / [[spec-rare-earth-statecraft]] / Taiwan.)

## 4. Aerospace whistleblowers (Boeing, 2024) — *official causes documented; suspicion is timing-driven*
- **John Barnett** — ex-Boeing quality manager, found dead **9 Mar 2024** in Charleston, SC, the day after a deposition in his retaliation suit. The **Charleston County Coroner + police** (reports released 17 May 2024) concluded **self-inflicted gunshot**; records note PTSD/anxiety. Family and counsel doubted it given the timing. *Grade: fact (official ruling) + contested (timing).*
- **Joshua Dean** — Spirit AeroSystems auditor and 737-MAX whistleblower; died **1 May 2024** (45) of a fast-spreading infection (reported **MRSA**); his mother requested an autopsy. *Grade: fact (illness); no foul-play evidence.*
- **The substantive issue** is the underlying **safety and retaliation allegations**, which stand on their own regardless of the deaths. Two related whistleblowers dying within ~2 months is a striking coincidence; no evidence of foul play has emerged. (Intersects accountability / [[macro-official-data-integrity]].)

## 5. Crypto figures (2022–2023) — *mostly base-rate coincidence*
The most over-hyped strand. **Nikolai Mushegian** (MakerDAO/DAI co-architect) drowned in Puerto Rico (28 Oct 2022) hours after a paranoid tweet; **Tiantian Kullander** (Amber Group co-founder) died in his sleep at 30 (Nov 2022); **Vyacheslav Taran** died in a helicopter crash (Nov 2022); others (Biosca, Pérez Algaba) involved unrelated crime/violence. Newsweek and others note **limited-to-no evidence of any plot** — heterogeneous causes (drowning / natural / aviation) are *not* a signature. This strand best illustrates the base-rate caveat. *Grade: labeled interpretation (mostly coincidence).* (Context for [[spec-onchain-threat-actor-addresses]].)

## 6. Synthesis — five clusters, five different truths
Strongest → weakest evidence: **(1)** Iranian nuclear scientists = documented, attributed state campaign · **(2)** Russian energy executives = real, sustained, statistically striking cluster, cause unestablished · **(3)** Boeing whistleblowers = official causes documented, suspicion timing-driven · **(4)** Chinese defense scientists = reported cluster, circumstances unverified, no perpetrator · **(5)** crypto figures = largely coincidence. The disciplined point is methodological: **"they keep disappearing" is a pattern-claim that must be decomposed and graded, not asserted as one conspiracy.**

## What is NOT asserted
- **No** single actor or coordinated campaign across these clusters.
- The Russian-executive and Chinese-scientist deaths are **unexplained clusters**, not established murders; official causes are distrusted but not disproven.
- **No** party is accused of causing the Boeing deaths — official causes are suicide (Barnett) and infection (Dean); the safety/retaliation allegations are the real issue.
- The crypto deaths are **not** asserted as foul play.
- Overlay edges (state-action / relationship / regulatory) are **excluded from the SCC / Z3 / TLA+ proofs.**
