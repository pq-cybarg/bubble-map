## 1. Jump & the manually-propped "algorithmic" peg

**Jump Crypto** (HFT giant **Jump Trading**'s digital arm; **Kanav Kariya** ran it) **secretly propped the TerraUSD peg** in May 2021 — buying UST to defend $1 in exchange for **deeply-discounted LUNA** — booking an alleged **~$1.28B** profit, then exiting before the May-2022 collapse. The **SEC charged Jump's subsidiary Tai Mo Shan ~$123M** for misleading investors about UST; the **CFTC** opened a probe; Terraform's administrator sued Jump for **~$4B**. The "algorithmic" stablecoin's "organic" peg was a **managed illusion** — verify the claim. *Fact.*
