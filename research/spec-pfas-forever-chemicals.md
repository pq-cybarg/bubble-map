## 1. The makers (and the liability spinoff)

PFAS = a class of ~15,000 **"forever chemicals."** Dominant makers: **3M** (the original) and **DuPont** — which spun its PFAS/materials liabilities into **Chemours** (2015) and split off **Corteva**. Spinning liabilities into a separate entity **echoes the liability-engineering playbook** (J&J Texas two-step / opioid bankruptcy). *Fact.*

## 2. The AFFF water settlements

Via **AFFF MDL 2873** (Charleston, SC): **3M** — up to **$10.3B over 13 yr** to public water suppliers (final approval **Mar 29 2024**); **Chemours/DuPont/Corteva** — combined **$1.185B** (~$592M/$400M/$193M, June 2023) + a 2025 NJ environmental settlement. These resolve **public-water-system** claims; personal-injury claims continue. *Fact.*

## 3. The suppression (the verification/cover-up core)

A peer-reviewed **Annals of Global Health** study: 3M and DuPont **actively suppressed PFAS hazard evidence from the 1960s.** DuPont knew **C-8 (PFOA) was "highly toxic"** by 1970; a 1979 study killed dogs in one dose; in **1980** the companies learned of **worker birth defects and hid it** (a 1981 memo denying any link). ProPublica documented **"toxic gaslighting."** Same suppress-the-evidence pattern as **Big Tobacco** and the **verification-collapse** theme. *Fact (internal records / the study).*

## 4. The chipmaking dependency (the cross-link)

The semiconductor industry has **1000+ uses** of PFAS (photoresists, rinses, etchants, coolants, seals); **SEMI calls them "essential"** and claims removing low-volume-exemption PFAS **"would shut down all US domestic semiconductor manufacturing"**; there are **no federal Clean Water Act effluent limits** on chipmaking PFAS discharges. **IMEC** is researching PFAS-free resists. So the **CHIPS-Act reshoring push collides with the PFAS-cleanup push.** *Uses/no-limit status fact; the "shutdown" claim is contested industry assertion.*

## 5. The 2025-26 EPA rollback (another reversal)

EPA's **Apr-2024** rule set limits for **6 PFAS** (PFOA/PFOS at 4 ppt). In **2025-26** the EPA proposed to **keep PFOA/PFOS but extend compliance to 2031**, and to **rescind** the limits for **PFHxS, PFNA, GenX, PFBS** (proposed May 18 2026; comments to Jul 20 2026). The protective rule **partially unwound on a political clock** — the same prosecuted/regulated-then-reversed pattern as the crypto/spyware/Adani files. *Fact.*

## 6. The honest reading

PFAS is the mass-tort that is **also a present-tense industrial dependency**: documented, suppressed, adjudicated harm + multibillion settlements on one side; genuine **essentiality** to chip fabrication (and firefighting foam, medical devices) on the other; and a **2025-26 rollback** unwinding part of the protection. Liability was **spun into separate entities**; cleanup lands on **water utilities and ratepayers**; and **chip-reshoring collides with cleanup**. Both true: real concealed harm AND a chemical the economy is genuinely hooked on.
