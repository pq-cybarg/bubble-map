## 1. Multi-alignment (the keystone)

India's revealed posture is **multi-alignment / strategic hedging** (stated structurally): it will **chair BRICS 2026** *and* sit in the **Quad** (US/Japan/Australia); it pursues **IMEC** (the US/EU/Gulf corridor explicitly positioned against China's BRI — though stalled, no firm funding) *and* benefits from the **China thaw**; it keeps buying **Russian oil** (**1.16M bpd**, Feb 2026, down from ~1.71M) *while* signing an **interim US trade deal**. **Overlapping memberships in rival blocs are the inconsistency.** *Fact.*

**The tariff episode:** the US imposed a **25% tariff (Aug 2025)** over Russian oil; Trump removed it claiming Modi would stop — but the joint statement **omitted** any commitment, and a SCOTUS ruling constrained the tariff power. Multi-alignment **survived coercion.** *Fact.*

## 2. The China thaw (tactical, not strategic)

After the post-Galwan freeze: **border-delimitation talks**, reopened border trade (Lipulekh/Shipki La/Nathu La), India relaxing **Press Note 3** investment curbs, and China **lifting rare-earth-magnet export restrictions** to India (Aug 2025). Analysts call it **tactical** (mutual hedging of US unpredictability), not reconciliation; the border rivalry persists. *Fact; "tactical" supported.*

## 3. The Adani layer (and another enforcement reversal)

**Nov 2024:** the US EDNY indicted **Gautam Adani** et al. for securities/wire fraud + a **~$250M bribery** scheme for solar contracts; the SEC filed a parallel case. By **2026 the US is moving to end** the cases (settlement talks ~$15-20M SEC, ~$275M OFAC) — the **prosecuted-then-de-prosecuted** pattern seen in the crypto/spyware files. **Adani** (ports/energy/airports/coal) and **Reliance** (Ambani — Jio/retail/energy) are India's conglomerate pillars. *Fact; presumption of innocence.*

## 4. Digital public infrastructure (model + concentration risk)

**UPI** processed **~22.35B transactions in April 2026** (more in a month than Visa does globally in a day; ~3.4% of GDP); **Aadhaar** has enrolled **~1.4B** people (the world's largest biometric ID). Celebrated for **inclusion** — but the **same scale** means one breach/outage hits hundreds of millions, and Aadhaar is the **biometric-ID analogue** of the digital-ID-orchestration concerns the corpus tracks. *Figures fact; concentration/surveillance read graded.*

## 5. Domestic politics

Modi's **BJP** consolidated in the **2026 state elections** (a historic first win in **West Bengal**; a third term in Assam), described as **"hegemonic"** with religious-polarisation campaigning. *Results fact; "backsliding" contested.*

## 6. The honest reading

India's **structural pattern is multi-alignment/hedging** — refusing capture by either bloc and extracting from all (Russian discount oil, US tech/trade, Chinese inputs, Gulf/IMEC capital) — which makes it the **keystone of the alignment-inconsistency thread.** Domestically: the world's largest **DPI** (inclusion + concentration risk) under an increasingly dominant **BJP**, with conglomerate champions whose US legal exposure is being **wound down**. Composition guard: institutional **action/incentives**, not a national feeling.
