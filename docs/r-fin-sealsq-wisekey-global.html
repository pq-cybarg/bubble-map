Re-grade

The story has matured from press-release velocity into a real, state-co-financed footprint — and government anchoring (Gujarat ISM, Spanish SETT.ES, EU Chips Act, US "Quantum Made in USA") is the same state-as-sponsor durability that lifts MP Materials/Centrus (defense-rare-earth) and IonQ (macro-quantum-computing) above pure narrative-beta. But the caution sharpens: the cash is raised-not-earned; the narrative stack grew from 3 → ~5 (quantum + space + crypto + sovereign-semiconductor-subsidy + digital-identity); most sources are vendor MoUs/LOIs (intentions); and real PQC silicon stays incumbent-led. Likelihood of becoming a major PQC/quantum principal: still LOW–MODERATE — the India JV is a genuine gradeable hedge, but certification + revenue must follow the announcements. Not part of the AI circular core (not in the SCC); a state-subsidized real-asset skeleton under a microcap narrative, and the WISeKey↔Hedera bridge that opens Task #2.
