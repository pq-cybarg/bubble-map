1. The party vertical

The CCP — not the government — holds ultimate authority. Xi holds the three crowns: General Secretary (party), President (state), CMC Chairman (army). The Politburo Standing Committee is the apex body; the State Council executes. Not monolithic: the Fourth Plenum (2025) purged ~10 Central Committee members — "policy continuity, personnel turmoil." Fact.

2. The state-owned economy

SASAC oversees the central SOEs at the commanding heights (energy/telecom/banks/rail/defense). The state is reasserting primacy: SOE transfers +55% in 2025 (post-2010 high) as private "animal spirits" stayed dampened. Inverts the chaebol/keiretsu model — here the state owns the champions outright. Fact.

3. Military-civil fusion

MCF — quietly re-emphasized in the 2025 plan — creates interoperable civilian-defense standards + a "green channel" routing civilian S&T (AI, chips, quantum, space) into the PLA (which reports to the CMC/Xi, not the state). This is what makes the map's China-AI, hardware, USTC-quantum, and SC42-standards nodes dual-use by policy. Policy fact; firm-by-firm transfer case-dependent — composition guard.

4. The debt counterweight

The strain the growth story hides: public-budget revenue fell for the first time in 30+ years (2025); aggregate deficit a record ~9% of GDP; the LGFV (local-government financing vehicle) debt mountain is the systemic risk. The 15th FYP cracks down on "involution" and reasserts the center over the provinces. Fact.

5. The yuan rail

The PBoC operates CIPS — the cross-border yuan rail Russia and Iran route oil-for-RMB through to bypass USD/SWIFT. Every sanctioned state pushed onto CIPS deepens the yuan bloc — but non-convertibility (capital controls) is both the ceiling and the leverage over captive users. PBoC/CIPS fact; "yuan bloc" interpretation.

6. The big-tech crackdown cycle

2020-22 disciplined the champions: Ant Group's record IPO pulled + forced restructure; DiDi forced to delist; Alibaba record antitrust fine; CAC asserted control (and now runs the AI-censorship layer). The cycle: crackdown → leash → selective rehabilitation. The inverse of the US "operator-network writes the rules" dynamic — here the party writes the rules and the champions comply. Fact.

7. The honest reading

A party-state where the CCP (Xi's three crowns) holds ultimate authority over a state-owned commanding-heights economy, channels civilian S&T into the military by policy (MCF), runs the yuan rail the sanctioned states settle through, and disciplines its private champions — all integrated by the Five-Year-Plan apparatus. Countervailing: a record deficit + LGFV debt, factional purges, and the convertibility ceiling on the yuan. The structural finding is concentration-by-design (the mirror of the West's emergent concentration) — with real fragilities, and the composition guard intact (factional, not monolithic).
