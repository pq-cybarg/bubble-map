Method

Research is split across research/*.json (structured edges + sources) and research/*.md (write-ups). models/graph/build_graph.py consolidates the edges into data/graph.json (150 nodes, 162 edges) and runs the Tarjan SCC. The Z3, TLA+, and Alloy models take their inputs from that data. Claims are graded fact · contested · weak · unsupported; graded overlays are kept out of the proofs. Intent is not inferred from adjacency. Reproduce with bash run_all.sh (Python + Z3; Java for TLA+/Alloy, jars auto-fetched).

What this is and is not

It is a cited inventory of how the AI, banking, credit, commodity, energy, defense, crypto, telecom, and identity webs connect, with the financial core machine-checked and the rest evidence-graded. It is not a claim of a single conspiracy: the reading is a recurring operator-network plus recurring structures (LTCM interconnection, Enron off-balance-sheet vehicles, dotcom vendor financing) plus regulatory arbitrage, rebuilt each era in the least-regulated venue. Sensitive threads (people, influence, foreign actors) are graded and excluded from the proofs.
