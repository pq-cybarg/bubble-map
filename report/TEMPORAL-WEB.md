- **E3 — 2020–2026 · the AI loop & the identity layer.** The circular AI-capex core; Stargate/Hyperion **SPVs**; **SVB fails (2023)** having been EGRRCPA-exempted; **GENIUS Act (2025)** legalizes stablecoins; SpaceX IPO (2026); the digital-ID push (TBI/BritCard, Worldcoin, Meta age-verification); **Summers resigns the OpenAI board over Epstein emails (Nov 2025)**.

The defensible critique is about **efficacy / technical understanding, not corruption**: a ranking member can be earnest and well-supported yet out-positioned by a far better-funded, better-coordinated industry apparatus (Fairshake PAC, the bank lobbies, the crypto exchanges). "Misinformed or out-resourced" fits the record; "malicious" does not.

The Epstein, Waters, and foreign-influence threads are graded associations, deliberately quarantined from the formal proofs.
